Smooth polylines

Smoothes a line to improve its aesthetic or cartographic quality. To do that the geoprocess computes the curve that best fit the coordinate sequence of the original geometry.

The kind of curves implemented are:
  1. BEZIER
  2. B-SPLINE
  3. CARDINAL SPLINE
  4. CATMULLROM SPLINE
  5. CUBIC B-SPLINE
  6. LAGRANGE
  7. NATURAL CUBIC SPLINE
  8. NURBS
