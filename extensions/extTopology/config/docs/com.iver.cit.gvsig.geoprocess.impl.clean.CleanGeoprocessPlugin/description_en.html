Clean

Generates a layer with topologically valid lines and polygons by correcting geometry errors, converting polylines to polygons and updating attribute table schemas where necessary.

In addition, the following clean-up processes are applied:

  1. Fusion of nodes that are closer than the specified fuzzy tolerance.
  2. Elimination of overshooting (dangling) line nodes (parameter dangle length)
  3. Identification of line node intersections (pseudo nodes), and creation of missing vertices at intersections.
