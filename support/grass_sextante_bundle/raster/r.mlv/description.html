DESCRIPTION

The Mean of Least Variance (MLV) filter is an edge-preserving (actually edge-enhancing) noise smoothing filter based on the concepts of mathematical morphology.

It uses moving subwindows of defined size. The result is the mean of the subwindow with minimal variance. There is size*size subwindows, in there the the variance must be compouted.
